# **App Name**: Egypt Bound 2026

## Core Features:

- Detailed Itinerary View: Present the day-by-day program for both Cairo and Sharm El Sheikh, including historical sights, cultural activities, and leisure options through an organized, tabbed interface.
- Interactive Booking Inquiry: Enable users to initiate a booking or request a personalized quote via a direct WhatsApp link with pre-filled message templates, simplifying the inquiry process.
- Departure Date Selector: Allow users to easily browse and select from available departure and return dates from Alger for their chosen travel package.
- Hotel & Pricing Overview: Display a comprehensive list of hotel options in Sharm El Sheikh with their respective pricing per person, star ratings, and accommodation types, along with details for Cairo accommodation.
- Flight Schedule Display: Provide detailed flight plans, including flight numbers, routes (e.g., Alger → Caire → Sharm), departure, and arrival times for each available travel date.
- AI Travel Assistant Tool: A generative AI chatbot tool that answers common questions about the Egypt tour, such as visa requirements, trip inclusions, hotel facilities, or offers quick summaries of daily activities from the itinerary.
- Practical Information & FAQ: Clearly present essential travel information including visa procedures, hotel check-in policies, room capacities, and agency contact details for inquiries.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Rich Gold (#D4A827) for a sense of luxury, historical significance, and warm desert sun, contrasting brilliantly with the dark background.
- Background color: Deep Sepia (#1A1916), a heavily desaturated, dark earthy tone, visually harmonized with the primary gold, reminiscent of ancient parchments and moonlit desert landscapes.
- Accent color: Muted Terracotta (#E5AD9F), analogous to the primary color, providing a soft, natural accent that evokes historical earthenware and sunsets, distinct in brightness and saturation.
- Headline font: 'Cormorant Garamond' (serif), chosen for its elegant and timeless character, perfectly conveying the historical grandeur of Egypt. Body font: 'DM Sans' (sans-serif), providing clear readability and a modern, professional feel. Note: currently only Google Fonts are supported.
- Stylized line-art icons that subtly incorporate Egyptian motifs, ensuring clarity and complementing the luxurious aesthetic while maintaining a modern, sophisticated presentation.
- A responsive, tabbed layout effectively organizing detailed trip information, prioritizing intuitive navigation and readability across all devices. Emphasize a clear visual hierarchy for itinerary segments, inclusions, and pricing.
- Subtle, fluid transitions and hover effects on interactive elements like tabs, cards, and buttons, enhancing user engagement with an elegant and seamless experience.
